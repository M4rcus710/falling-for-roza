Red Shirt is our first EP—and it’s everything we are, poured into five tracks. We wrote, recorded, and released it all DIY, with zero shortcuts and all heart. It’s loud, raw, sometimes rough around the edges, but always honest. These songs tell our stories—the loud ones, the quiet ones, and everything in between.

Leash was the first track we ever released. It set the tone for what we wanted to do—gritty rock with energy and edge. It’s where our sound started taking shape, and it still means a lot to us.

Made My Bed dives into the feeling of being stuck with the choices you've made—knowing you’re the one who put yourself there, but not sure how to move on. It’s one of our moodier tracks, built on tension, space, and a slow burn that hits when it needs to.
